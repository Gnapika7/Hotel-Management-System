SERVICES

   GYM

                    Our hotel gym is designed to help you stay active and energized
  throughout your stay. Equipped with modern fitness machines including 
  treadmills, weights, and exercise bikes, it offers everything you need for
  a complete workout. Guests can enjoy a clean, spacious environment with 
  complimentary water, towels, and locker facilities, making it easy to maintain 
  your fitness routine while traveling. Open daily with flexible hours, the gym
  ensures you never have to compromise on health and wellness during your visit.

   
     Shopping Arcade

                    Our hotel’s shopping arcade brings convenience and variety right
                    to your doorstep. Guests can explore a selection of boutique stores
                    offering fashion, accessories, souvenirs, and daily essentials without
                    leaving the property. Whether you need a quick purchase, a
                    memorable gift, or simply want to browse, the arcade provides a 
                    comfortable and stylish shopping experience designed to meet your
                    needs during your stay.

   24/7 Room Services

                    Our hotel offers round‑the‑clock room service to ensure comfort and
convenience at any hour. Guests can enjoy freshly prepared meals, snacks,
and beverages delivered directly to their rooms, whether it’s an early
breakfast, a late‑night craving, or a special request. With options to 
suit different tastes and dietary needs, our attentive staff is always 
ready to provide a seamless dining experience without leaving the comfort
 of your room.
