About Us

Spend your Dream Holidays... 

 
            What is the one thing you desire, as a modern traveler? Isn’t it a 
place you can come home to, even when you are a long way from home? This  
is where Sunrise comes in. We bring you beautiful apartments that merge  
design and comfort of boutique hotels, with  the  local  ambiance and  
experience of vacation rental. Conceptualized  as  an  apart - hotel, 
Sunrise unlocks for you designer furnishing, fully-equipped kitchens and
living rooms, and the amenities of your favorite hotel, in the midst of 
the city you are traveling to!

            Whether you are a solo traveler, couple, family, remote worker,
long-term renter, business traveler, or cultural enthusiast, OneTree offers
you decadent spaces that can be called home right from the get-go. Now, 
live like a local in our apartments located in the most exciting and 
best-connected neighborhoods. At Sunrise, our vision is simple – to 
redefine the way people travel and explore locations, by transforming your
living spaces into stunning works of art.
